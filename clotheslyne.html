About Us

The retail industry is the second largest pollutant in the world after the oil industry. Clotheslyne's circular fashion based platform uses preloved clothing, helping to reduce textile waste and carbon dioxide emissions. We are here to help customers clean out their closets and conveniently stay in style in a sustainable fashion and at a fraction of the retail price.
